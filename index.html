Who we are

We are family company who has high enthusiasm toward Indonesian Tobacco which is acknowledged by the world because it has an unusual flavor than the other tobaccos around the world. In addition, we produce the best quality of handmade cigars in many sizes and types. These are Cigarillos, Panatela, Corona, Grand Corona, Robusto, Grand Robusto, Super Robusto, Edmundo, Churchill, and many others.

100% uses the original Indonesian Tobaccos. This is the advantage and our pride to acknowledge unusual flavor of Indonesian Tobacco toward Cigar Lovers around the world. We make a cigar with premium flavor. It is different but classy and has great design for the packaging, so that it can introduce the local wisdom of Indonesia that is called “Mahakarya Putra Indonesia” in each inch of Sultan Cigar Indonesia.

The history

Our father is the best blending master

It began in 2009, a Father blended local Indonesian Tobaccos for creating an unusual cigar which was different to cigars in the market demand, and also for introducing new cigar blending with Indonesian flavor. At that time, he sold and promoted the product conventionally to some colleagues and cigar companies.

At the first time, we sold the product without a band identity, because they would sell the product with their own brand identity which was acknowledge well in the market. By done it more and more with huge spirit and consistency, this cigar was welcomed and to be a pioneer in Indonesia. Because of that, we saw huge opportunities to create our own product which could be sold inside and outside of the country. Through Sultan Cigar Indonesia company, we tried to develop some brands of cigar to reach more segments.

Our Business

Retail cigar creator

We are the owner of Sultan Cigar which is promoted in many countries in Europe and America. Sultan Cigar become the pioneer of Indonesian flavor which is originally from Indonesia’s land, so there is no single other tobaccos from other countries. Through the tagline “Mahakarya Putra Indonesia”, we try to present a different flavor of cigar which can be found only in Sultan Cigar.

Custom cigar creator

Sultan Cigar Indonesia company also serves the special order for business needed. There are many cigar brands in Indonesia using our service to make their own product. Definitely, it is different with Sultan’s flavor. Also, we are so welcome to serve a merchandise or gift to build an intimacy relationship for business partners or friendships.
