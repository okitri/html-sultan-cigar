Who we are

We are family company who has high enthusiasm toward Indonesian Tobacco which is acknowledged by the world because it has an unusual flavor than the other tobaccos around the world. In addition, we produce the best quality of handmade cigars in many sizes and types. These are Cigarillos, Panatela, Corona, Grand Corona, Robusto, Grand Robusto, Super Robusto, Edmundo, Churchill, and many others.

100% uses the original Indonesian Tobaccos. This is the advantage and our pride to acknowledge unusual flavor of Indonesian Tobacco toward Cigar Lovers around the world. We make a cigar with premium flavor. It is different but classy and has great design for the packaging, so that it can introduce the local wisdom of Indonesia that is called “Mahakarya Putra Indonesia” in each inch of Sultan Cigar Indonesia.
