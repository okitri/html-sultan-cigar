Sultan Cigar Indonesia was born as a company family which produces cigars. This company was established in 18th February 2009, and officialy registered as a company in Ministry of Trade of Republic Indonesia in 2011. Furthermore, the brand identity of Sultan Cigar was officialy founded in 2014.

We have good relationship and do "production contract" with several factories in Indonesia which produce our cigars under good supervision for the shake of keeping the best quality of Sulatan Cigar Indonesia.
